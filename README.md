# 📱 Mobile Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Overview

The Mobile Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze mobile phone sales performance across India. It provides insights into sales trends, customer ratings, payment methods, product performance, and regional sales distribution through dynamic visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Compare sales across cities.
- Identify top-selling mobile models.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Analyze payment method preferences.
- Evaluate customer satisfaction ratings.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### KPI Cards
- Total Sales
- Total Quantity Sold
- Total Transactions
- Average Sales

### Sales Analysis
- Sales by City (Map)
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales by Mobile Model
- Sales by Day of Week

### Customer Analysis
- Customer Ratings
- Payment Method Distribution

### Interactive Filters
- Mobile Model
- Brand
- Payment Method
- Day Name
- Month
